Danish Ali (violinist)

Danish started his initial training from his grandfather and his uncle late ustad ahmed ali khan, who was an ‘a’ grade artist of all india radio and great sirat player, after their unfortunate demise, he started continiously violin traning from his uncle shri aleem khan who is a great violinst.

And taking ‘gayiki ang’ from ustad iqbal ahmed khan (khalifa of delhi gharana). Danish ali got first prize in many prestigious competitions like harballabh sangeet sammelan music competition and many more. He got scholarship from ministry of india in 2007. Also he has performed in many prestigious festivals’s in india and abroad.

Glen Fernandes (Keyboard Player)

Glen is a singer, keyboard player. He attended Trinity School & Berkley school of music.

Glen was regularly accompanied bands and choirs in school and church as kid. During his teens he was introduced to his first jazz records and a lifelong fascination was sparked. He studied Jazz piano performance given by renowned artist. Glen has been teaching keyboard and playing in many bars and hotels all around the globe.He has played at iconic stages & various venues.
Glen also regularly helps out in various youth and social projects.

Hafeez Ahmed (Tabla)

Born in family of great musician of Delhi Gharana he is the son of Ustad Sayyed Ahmed Alvi who is an ‘a’ grade artist of All India radio and a well known sitar player.

He has been learing tabla under the guidence of Ustad Akram Khan of Ajarara Gharana and has won many prestigious competitions like; All India Radio music competition, Haribalabh Sangeet Sammelan Music Competition, Lucknow Sangeet Natak Academy music competition and many more.

About OzoneRaaga

Ozone is a fusion band based in New Delhi India, which contain pure classical music from Indian culture and a fragrance of western music in it. Since the group was formed, in 2009, Ozone has steadily grown to become one of the most respected acts on the Indian music scene.

Our sound is a mélange of the styles, moods and textures we have taken in from different cultures and different music styles of the world. Ozone is inspired by the famous band called ‘SHAKTI’. In our own way, we attempt to dissolve into the world we perceive, and discover music that resonates around us.

Many great musicians, especially in the Indian Classical arena, speak of the pursuit of that incredible moment during performance when the musician becomes the music and forgets his own identity. In our composition you will find the mixture of ragas with combination of western music together.
